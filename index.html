Why it matters

Workflow definitions should not collapse into prompt soup or diagram-only artifacts. WebProcess treats process design as a real specification surface.

What it aims for

Clear semantics, modular resources, durable execution, and a model that can express retries, approvals, waits, loops, and AI-driven decision paths.
